Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-14

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how RealEstateAI (the "Service") collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use our website, web application, APIs, and integrations.

2. Information We Collect

Account information: name, email, role, organization/workspace identifiers, authentication data (hashed passwords), and security metadata (session version, device identifiers).

Lead and conversation data: messages, contact details, properties requested, budget/location/timeline, channel source, and structured fields created from message parsing.

Usage and diagnostics: event logs, audit logs, IP address (where provided by your network), request identifiers, rate-limit events, and performance metrics.

Billing metadata: subscription identifiers and customer identifiers from payment processors (we do not store full card data).

Integration credentials: tokens and secrets you provide for channels (stored and used to connect your accounts). You are responsible for providing correct credentials and rotating them when needed.

3. How We Use Information

We use information to operate the Service, including: ingesting leads, deduplicating and enriching records, generating analytics, sending follow-ups you configure, scheduling appointments, and producing exports/reports.

We also use information to secure the Service: fraud prevention, access control, rate limiting, anomaly detection, and incident response.

4. Legal Bases (Where Applicable)

Depending on your jurisdiction, processing may be based on contract performance, legitimate interests (security, prevention of abuse), and consent (where you enable marketing or messaging automations).

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We share information with:

  • Service providers that help us host and operate the Service (infrastructure, monitoring, email delivery, payment processing).
  • Integration providers you connect (e.g., messaging platforms) to send/receive messages or events.
  • Legal and safety disclosures when required by law, or to protect rights, security, and users.

We do not sell personal information.

6. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide the Service and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your workspace data subject to contractual and legal constraints.

7. Security

We use security measures such as hashed passwords, role-based access control, rate limiting, request identifiers, transport security (TLS where available), and audit logging.

No system can be guaranteed 100% secure. You are responsible for using strong passwords, protecting integration credentials, and restricting workspace access.

8. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries different from where you live, depending on infrastructure providers. Where applicable, we use appropriate safeguards.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, export, delete, or restrict processing of your information. You can contact us to exercise these rights.

10. Changes

We may update this Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
